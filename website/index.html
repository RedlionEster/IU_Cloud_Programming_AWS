What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, particularly computer systems. AI applications range from expert systems to natural language processing, speech recognition, and computer vision.

Popular Use Cases of AI

Image Recognition

AI systems can identify and classify objects in images, aiding in areas like medical diagnosis and facial recognition.

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

NLP enables computers to understand and interpret human language, making chatbots and language translators possible.

Recommendation Systems

AI recommends products, movies, or songs based on user behavior, as seen on platforms like Netflix and Amazon.
